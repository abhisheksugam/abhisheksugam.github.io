About Me

Engineer. Builder. Problem Solver.

I'm a Product Design Lead and Systems Architect who transforms early-stage ideas into products that ship at scale.

I live in the space between a napkin sketch and a million-unit production line. At Amazon, I lead product design for Alexa-powered devices -- defining system architecture, orchestrating cross-functional teams, and making the thousands of decisions that turn a bold concept into something you can unbox and fall in love with.

My craft is taking ambiguity and turning it into clarity: translating customer needs into mechanical architectures, resolving impossible trade-offs between form and function, and relentlessly iterating until every detail earns its place. From first principles through final tooling, I own the full arc of bringing hardware to life.
