WHO AM I?

I am a Graduate student at University of Washington, Seattle who designs and builds new products, devices and systems. I am majoring in Mechanical engineering with a focus on Product development, design(CAE/FEA) and Manufacturing.

I have always been interested in developing new devices, I have designed, prototyped and manufactured consumer ready devics, autonomus robots, All Terrain Vehicles, Formula One Car and a hybrid Chevrolet Camaro.

I dedicate a lot of my time on Finite element analysis, performance testing and physical testing of the prototypes to ensure product quality. I have significant manufacturing and rapid prototyping experience through my academic projects and internships. I enjoy working on open ended problems, end-to-end product design and development, optimizations through finite element analysis (FEA), rapid Prototyping. I also enjoy creating the manufacturing strategy for a consumer ready product.

Together, we can leverage my diverse experiences working on real-world mechanical design and manufacturing to create products and grow your business
